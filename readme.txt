Sellfy is the simplest way to sell digital downloads using WordPress blog. Embed individual products or create your own store for digital products.

== Description ==

[Sellfy](https://sellfy.com/?utm_source=wordpress+plugin "Sellfy - Sell digital downloads") is an e-commerce platform for selling digital downloads directly to your audience. Upload and sell ebooks, videos, music, comics, design or anything else digital you create. [Sellfy](https://sellfy.com/?utm_source=wordpress+plugin "Sellfy - Sell digital downloads") will list your products and automatically deliver them to customers after the purchase.

= Features =
* Connect with most popular payment methods - PayPal, Stripe and Paymill;
* We do not limit total storage, bandwidth and you can have unlimited number of products
* Use powerful marketing tools to increase your sales

If you don't have Sellfy account, create one [here](https://sellfy.com/signup/?utm_source=wordpress+plugin "Register to Sellfy - Sell digital downloads")

== Installation ==
1. Upload wp-sellfy.zip through Plugins->Add New, or extract and upload the folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory
1. Activate the plugin in the 'Plugins' menu
1. Navigate to now visible 'Sellfy' menu after 'Tools'
1. Fill out a simple form
